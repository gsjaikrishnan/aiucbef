WELFARE SCHEMES FOR RETIRED EMPLOVEES

Group Mediclaim Insurance for Retired Employees: The Scheme for retired employees and spouse provides for reimbursement of hospitalization expenses to the extent of Rs. 2.00 lac (Circular CHO/PMG/19/2012-13 dt.26.9.2012). Scheme for reimbursement of further hospitalization expenses against uncovered hospitalization bills with a maximum of Rs.25000 in case of Retired Employee and Rs.10000 in case of spouse of retired employee, in connection with treatment of Heart Surgery, Brain Tumor, Kidney failure, Cancer and major surgery in the event of any accident. The scheme will be effective from Nov 1, 2012.

Eligibility: All normal / Voluntary Retired Employees who are covered under Group Mediclaim Insurance.

Ref No. CHO/PMG/28/2012-13 DT 20.11.2012.
