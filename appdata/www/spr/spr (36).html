PART TIME EMPLOYEES :

Part time employees in 1/3 rd scale wages and above are eligible for the following :

  • Leave benefits

  • Medical Aid

  • Stagnation Increment

  • Hospitlisation expenses [pro rata]

  • LFC [pro rata]

  • Uniforms

  • Provident Fund

  • Gratuity

  • Pension

  • Leave Encashment on retirement

  • New Pension Scheme [for those who join from 1.4.2010]
