Rs.1210/-p.m. after they complete 3 years

Rs.1620/-p.m. after they complete 4 years

Rs.2010/-p.m. after they complete 5 years.

(1)Those who pass Graduation or CAIIB II when they are in 19th stage will be given one increment and one PQP from the date of passing and balance 4 installments of PQP in one year intervals thereafter.

(2)Those who pass Graduation/JAIIB/CAIIB after reaching 20th stage will be given 1st PQP from date of passing and balance 4 installments of PQP in one year intervals thereafter.

(3)Direct Graduation/Post graduation will be treated as Graduation and given 2 increments.
