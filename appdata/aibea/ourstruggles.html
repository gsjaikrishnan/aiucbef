OUR STRUGGLES:

AIBEA and Bank employees are known for fighting their way to improve their wages and service conditions. But that is only a part of our struggles. At every point of time, AIBEA has fought against the anti-people policies of the Government. In the 1960s AIBEA fought and achieved nationalisation of Banks. For the past more than two decades, AIBEA is consistently fighting against the neo-liberal economic policies and attempts to privatise the Public Sector Banks. Here is the catalogue of the strikes undertaken by AIBEA from 1991 ever since the liberalisation policies were announced and are being pursued by the Government.

7. 29.09.1994 Against economic policies of Government. Joint call by AIBEA, NCBE, BEFI, AIBOC
